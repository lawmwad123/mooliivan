About MOOLI IVAN

Experienced Leadership for Manafwa District

Personal Background

Born on Sunday, December 19, 1993, along the banks of River Manafwa in Bubwaya Ward, Manafwa Town, to Mwalimu Perez and Peninah Wanabunda. A true son of Manafwa District with deep roots and understanding of our community's needs and aspirations.

MOOLI IVAN is married to Alice Martha Mooli, and together they have been blessed by God with two children: Asiphiwe and Amma. As a devoted family man, he understands the importance of creating a better future for all families in Manafwa District.

Educational Journey

He began his education at Namatala Primary School, proceeded to Mbale Secondary School for both UCE and UACE, and later joined the Islamic University in Uganda for higher studies. A well-rounded education that has equipped him with the knowledge and skills needed for effective leadership.

Leadership Experience

2005: Library and Academic Prefect, Namatala Primary School
2008: Deputy Speaker, Wildlife Club, Mbale Secondary School
2009: Speaker, Mbale Secondary School, Boys Hostel
2011: Speaker, Pan-African Movement, Mbale Secondary School
2013: Guild Public Relations Officer, Islamic University in Uganda
2014: Chairperson Disaster Preparedness Bamasaba Students Association, Islamic University in Uganda
2015–2016: Guild Cabinet Minister for General Duties, Islamic University in Uganda
"My commitment is to serve with integrity, transparency, and dedication to ensure every citizen of Manafwa District benefits from quality leadership and development. Having been born and raised along the banks of River Manafwa, I understand our community's challenges and have the experience to lead us towards prosperity."
- MOOLI IVAN

Our Manifesto

Vision for Manafwa District 2026-2031

Infrastructure Development

  • Improve road networks connecting all sub-counties
  • Enhance water and sanitation facilities
  • Expand electricity access to rural areas
  • Construct modern markets and trading centers

Agriculture & Food Security

  • Support farmers with modern farming techniques
  • Establish agricultural processing centers
  • Improve access to quality seeds and fertilizers
  • Develop irrigation systems for year-round farming

Healthcare Services

  • Upgrade health center facilities and equipment
  • Recruit and retain qualified medical personnel
  • Improve maternal and child health services
  • Enhance disease prevention and health education

Education Excellence

  • Construct additional classrooms and schools
  • Provide learning materials and equipment
  • Support teacher training and development
  • Promote technical and vocational education

Youth & Women Empowerment

  • Create youth employment and skills programs
  • Support women's economic empowerment initiatives
  • Establish youth innovation and technology hubs
  • Promote gender equality in leadership

Good Governance

  • Ensure transparency in all district operations
  • Strengthen accountability mechanisms
  • Promote citizen participation in decision-making
  • Fight corruption at all levels
